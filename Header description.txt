Header descriptions for .dat files "Deployment One 6.25 to 6.50 hours" and "Deployment One 7.50 to 7.75 hours" for use with "importVector.m" and "motionCorrectVector3.m" Matlab scripts.
Data was measured at 64hz. Note that each file is padded with 3840 samples (one minute) at the beginning and end.

6	Amplitude (Beam1)		(counts)
7	Amplitude (Beam2)		(counts)
8	Amplitude (Beam3)		(counts)
9	SNR (Beam1)			(dB)

11	SNR (Beam3)			(dB)
